About Us

CLUB20 is a tier-5 student organization at Lovely Professional University well known for conducting sports, including tournaments, adventurous and recreational activities, and social events. We, at CLUB20, aim at promoting social, physical, and mental wellness, hunt talent in the university, providing them the path to success, and collect funds to donate for the treatment of cancer patients through our annual marathon “Race for Grace”. Our many events have been a tremendous success and are now branded in LPU.
We are not just a sporty organization, there’s a lot more! We believe in having fun and so we organize fun events frequently which are entertaining for both, the participants and the coordinators. This is just a drop in the ocean. It is with the incessant hard work and strong determination that we are being promoted to a level every year.
